ABOUT US

I am a Christchurch based Makeup Artist with over four years experience. Having trained in Christchurch and graduating with distinction I went straight into freelancing and working for Estee Lauder Companies as a Counter Manager. With the company I received further training to enhance my skillset. From there I went on to pursue freelancing work further and have since enjoyed being a part of many weddings and special occasions. My passion in makeup is the unique features of the individual and accentuate your natural beauty.

F.A.Q

Are you a qualified makeup artist?

Yes, I have a diploma in makeup artistry from KSSM. I have also been working in the beauty industry the past few years and have been trained through Clinique.

Where is the makeover done?

I have a studio set up at my home in the Sydenham/Beckenham area that I do makeovers in. I do travel for group bookings or for weddings with a 75cents per km travel fee outside of Christchurch Central or my residential area.

Do you travel outside of Christchurch?

Yes I do upon request. Travel charges of 0.75cents per km outside of the Christchurch zone apply.

Do I need to make a deposit?

A non refundable deposit of $20 is required for each special occasion appointment but is taken off your final cost. A non refundable deposit of $100 is required for wedding appointments but is taken off your final cost.

How far in advance should I book?

I advise booking as far in advance for your appointment as possible to ensure you don’t miss out. I take bookings as far as two years in advance.

When is the best time to have a trial?

I recommend 4-8 weeks before your wedding. This allows time to finalise the kind of look you want for your big day. A lot of brides find they change their minds on what they want after the trial, that is absolutely fine too, it helps them confirm exactly what they want for the wedding.

How long does the makeover take?

Allow 45-90 minutes depending on your appointment type and the look you want. Allow extra time for wedding trials as this is also a consultation to ensure you get the look you’re after.
